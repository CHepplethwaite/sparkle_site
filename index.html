fissure sealants

Fissure sealants are applied to the deep grooves and pits (fissures) on the chewing surfaces of the back teeth. These areas can be difficult to clean thoroughly with a toothbrush, making them more prone to the accumulation of bacteria and food particles.

Process

STEP ONE

The dentist begins by introducing themselves and creating a friendly, welcoming atmosphere to help ease any anxiety your child may have. We conduct an initial assessment of your child's oral health, asking about any concerns or issues your child might have.

Sparkle Dental

STEP 2

The dentist performs a thorough examination of your child's teeth, gums, and overall oral health. This may include checking for cavities, assessing bite alignment, and monitoring the development of permanent teeth. During the examination, the dentist educates your child on proper oral hygiene practices, including the importance of brushing, flossing, and regular dental check-ups. We use child-friendly tools and language to make the information more accessible.

To conclude the visit on a positive note, We provide positive reinforcement to your child for good oral hygiene practices. This could involve praising them for maintaining healthy habits or overcoming any fears during the check-up. As a special gesture, We present the child with a colorful and personalized certificate for being a "Super Smiler" or a "Cavity Fighter." This certificate serves as a fun and encouraging way to reinforce positive behavior and make the dental experience enjoyable for the child. The certificate may include the child's name, a cheerful message, and even a small illustration related to dental health. This small token of recognition helps create a positive association with dental visits and motivates your child to continue practicing good oral hygiene.
